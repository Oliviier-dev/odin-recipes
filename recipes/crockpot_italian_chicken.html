Crockpot Italian Chicken

Description

Crockpot Italian Chicken is a flavorful dish made with tender chicken, aromatic herbs, and a savory tomato-based sauce. It's a convenient and delicious meal that can be prepared in a slow cooker.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts or thighs
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Italian herbs and spices like basil, oregano, and thyme
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional: vegetables like bell peppers or mushrooms

Steps

  1. Place the chicken breasts or thighs in the crockpot/slow cooker.
  2. Add chopped onions and minced garlic to the slow cooker.
  3. In a bowl, combine tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes with Italian herbs such as basil, oregano, and thyme. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Pour the tomato sauce mixture over the chicken in the slow cooker.
  5. If desired, add additional vegetables like bell peppers or mushrooms to the slow cooker.
  6. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or on high heat for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  7. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.
  8. Return the shredded chicken back to the slow cooker and mix it with the sauce.
  9. Allow the chicken to simmer in the sauce for an additional 15-30 minutes to enhance the flavors.
  10. Serve the crockpot Italian chicken over cooked pasta, rice, or with crusty bread. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired.

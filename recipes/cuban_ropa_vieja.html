Cuban Ropa Vieja

Description

Cuban Ropa Vieja is a classic Cuban dish known for its tender shredded beef cooked in a flavorful tomato-based sauce, often served with rice and black beans.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds flank steak
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Season the flank steak with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. In a large skillet or Dutch oven, heat some olive oil over medium-high heat. Sear the steak on both sides until browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add more olive oil if needed. Saut√© the chopped onions, green bell pepper, and garlic until they become soft and fragrant.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, cumin, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir to combine.
  5. Return the seared flank steak to the skillet. Pour in the beef broth and add the bay leaves. Cover and simmer over low heat for about 2-3 hours or until the beef is tender and shreds easily.
  6. Once the beef is tender, remove it from the skillet and shred it using two forks.
  7. Return the shredded beef to the skillet and simmer for an additional 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  8. Garnish with chopped cilantro before serving.
